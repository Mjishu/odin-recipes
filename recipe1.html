Katsu Curry

For the Breading

  • 100g : flour
  • 1 : egg
  • 200g: Panko
  • 4 : boneless chicken Breasts
  • 100ml: vegetable oil

For the Curry Sauce

  • 1tbps : vegetable oil
  • 1 : onion(chopped)
  • 5: whole garlic cloves
  • 2: carrots
  • 2tbsp: flour
  • 1tbps: curry powder
  • 600ml: chicken stock
  • 2tsp: honey
  • 1tbps: soy sauce
  • 1: bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp: garam masala

Directions

  1. Sautee Garlic and Onion, add carrots cook slowly for 10 minutes. Stir Occasionly
  2. Add flour and curry powder, cook 1 minute; add stock while stirring, add honey soy sauce
    and bay leaf and boil. bring to a slow simmer and cook for 20 minutes add garam masala and pass sauce through sieve
  3. Lay flour,egg and breadcrumbs on seperate plates and coat the chicken in the order they were layed down in
  4. Heat oil in pan, fry the chicken until cooked, plate the chicken and finally add sauce the sauce drizzled over
